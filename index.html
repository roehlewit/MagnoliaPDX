About Us

Magnolia Remodel & Woodworks, LLC. serves the Portland Metro Area. We pride ourselves on our professionalism, up-front honesty, and the love of the work for the wonderful clients we serve. We go to great lengths to ensure that clients receive the end product they want and deserve.
Alex Rosenberg has been fascinated by building and construction since he can remember. After many years, and as many jobs, he found the right time to follow his passion with carpentry and woodworking. For the last several years he has been working whole house remodels, additions, kitchens bathrooms, decks and fences, doors and windows.
